Three layers, one source of truth.

Five tiers · The chain

Five roles, one chain. Everything passes through the gate.

Every task — from "send this venue reply" to "audit our advertising infrastructure" — flows through the same chain. Light AI for triage. Heavy AI for strategy. A quality gate before anything reaches the human. The roles map cleanly across whatever AI you bring.

Intake

The front door. Triages: what is this, what does it need, who handles it. Drafts a session brief.

Master

Senior strategist. Reads memory, claims the role, builds the plan. Reserved for decisions that matter.

Senior

Executor. Builds the draft, runs the audit, fixes infrastructure. The heavy lifting.

Quality

The gate. Grammar, accuracy, tone, link integrity, PII scan. Fails route back; only clean work ships.

Junior

The filer. Files to the right folder, appends session state, presents the deliverable to the human.

Journey · How we got here

Two years of nights, contractor disasters, and refusing to quit.

MasCom was not assembled in a sprint. It was iterated on through hundreds of sessions, debugged through real failures, and refined under real production load. Below is the spine of that build.

JUN 2024
Holding company formed
26 Cents LLC. Foundational, not flashy. The legal structure under everything that follows.
AUG 2025
First property under restoration
A historic building on the literal main street of a small town. Saw it as a wedding venue, not a fixer-upper.
FALL 2025
Contractor disaster — $40K lost
Half the renovation budget went to a contractor who never delivered. Lessons carved in scar tissue.
JAN 2026
First AI session — chaos
Hallucinated suggestions, broken tracking tags, advertising spent against ghost data. The system needed structure.
FEB 2026
Role cascade designed
Five tiers, one chain. Intake, Master, Senior, Quality, Junior. Nobody verifies their own work.
MAR 2026
Flagship venue launched
First wedding inquiry processed end-to-end through the new system in 6 minutes. Before: 35 minutes.
APR 2026
Three-layer architecture locked
Markdown source-of-truth · JSON graph · HTML interface. AI travels by warping, humans click anything.
MAY 2026
Public proof-of-concept live
Forensic seal scoring on every claim. Voice-intake onboarding designed. Auto-improvement loop in flight. Anyone can run their own.
Why it exists · The Restoration Architecture

Buildings, systems, and the discipline to rebuild both.

MasCom did not start as a software project. It started as a working system one person built to keep a real venue running, real properties operating, and real customers heard — without hiring out roles he could not afford to fill. He restores buildings as a literal job. He built MasCom so the systems behind those buildings could be restored too.

"The buildings were broken. The systems were broken. All have been rebuilt from the foundation up. That is not a coincidence. That is architecture." — from the build journal

MasCom is, at its core, a system of organized hope: that if you build the right structure, put the right guardrails in place, and refuse to cut corners even when you're exhausted — the work will hold. Now anyone with a real role and a real workload can run their own.
